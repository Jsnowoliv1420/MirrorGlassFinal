How We Work: An Overview
Window Installation
  • Preparation
    Measurements: Measure the size of the window frame and the opening to ensure the new window will fit properly.

    Tools and Materials: Prepare the necessary tools, such as a jigsaw, drill, level, tape measure, silicone sealant, anchors, screws, and the window.

    Remove the Old Window
    Disassembly: Remove the existing window by taking off any trim, molding, or screws holding it in place.

    Cleaning: Clean the frame and opening to ensure there are no residues or remnants of sealant.

    Installing the New Window
    Prepare the Frame: Place the new window in the opening to check the fit. Use shims to hold the window in place and level.

    Secure the Window: Secure the window using appropriate screws and anchors. Ensure it is level and properly aligned.

    Sealing: Apply silicone sealant around the frame to prevent water and air leaks.

    Replace Trim: Reinstall the trim and molding around the window.

    Final Check
    Check Functionality: Open and close the window to ensure it operates correctly.

    Cleaning: Clean any excess sealant or debris.
Mirror Installation
  • Preparation
    Measurements: Measure the area where the mirror will be installed and the size of the mirror.

    Tools and Materials: Prepare a level, drill, anchors, screws, tape measure, and hooks or mirror brackets.

    Area Preparation
    Mark the Location: Mark the spot on the wall where the mirror will be installed.

    Install Supports: Install the hooks or brackets on the wall, ensuring they are level.

    Placing the Mirror
    Hang the Mirror: With the help of another person, hang the mirror on the hooks or brackets.

    Adjust: Adjust the mirror to ensure it is level.

    Final Check
    Check Security: Ensure that the mirror is securely mounted and in place.

    Cleaning: Clean the mirror to remove any smudges or fingerprints.
Glass Door Installation
  • Preparation
    Measurements: Measure the door frame and the opening to ensure a proper fit.

    Tools and Materials: Have tools such as a drill, screws, hinges, handles, and the glass door ready.

    Frame Preparation
    Adjust the Frame: If necessary, adjust the frame to fit the dimensions of the glass door.

    Install Hinges: Attach the hinges to the door frame and the wall frame.

    Door Installation
    Place the Door: Position the glass door onto the hinges. You may need assistance to lift the door into place.

    Adjust: Adjust the hinges to ensure the door opens and closes smoothly.

    Installing Handles and Locks
    Attach Handles: Install the handles on the glass door according to the manufacturer's instructions.

    Install Locks: If the door requires a lock, install it in the appropriate position.

    Final Check
    Check Functionality: Open and close the door to ensure it operates correctly and fits well.

    Cleaning: Clean the glass door to remove any smudges or fingerprints.

    Additional Considerations
    Safety: Always use personal protective equipment, such as gloves and safety glasses, when working with glass and tools.

    Leveling: Ensure that all installations are level and properly aligned to avoid future issues.

    Check Local Regulations: Ensure compliance with any local regulations or building codes for installations.
