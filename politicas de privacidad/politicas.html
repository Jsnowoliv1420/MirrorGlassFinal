Privacy Policy

Last updated: 08/02/24

Brickell Mirror & Glass is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, and safeguard the personal information you provide when visiting our website.

1. Information We Collect

We collect personal information when you:

  • Visit our website: We collect browsing data such as your IP address, browser type, and the duration of your visit.
  • Fill out a contact form: We collect your name, email address, and any other information you choose to provide.
  • Subscribe to our newsletter: We collect your email address.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Provide and improve our services: To respond to your inquiries and offer you a personalized experience.
  • Send you communications: Including updates about our services, promotions, and news.
  • Analyze site usage: To improve the content and functionality of our website.

3. Sharing of Information

We do not share your personal information with third parties, except in the following circumstances:

  • With service providers: Who help us operate our website and conduct our business, as long as these providers agree to maintain the confidentiality of the information.
  • For legal requirements: When required by law or to protect our rights.

4. Information Security

We implement technical and organizational security measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, alteration, and destruction.

5. Cookies and Similar Technologies

Our website uses cookies and similar technologies to enhance your browsing experience. Cookies are small data files stored on your device. You can adjust your browser settings to refuse cookies, but this may limit your access to certain functionalities of the site.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access and update your personal information.
  • Request the deletion of your personal information, to the extent permitted by law.
  • Object to the processing of your information for marketing purposes.

To exercise these rights, you can contact us using the details provided below.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We recommend that you review this page periodically to stay informed about any changes.
